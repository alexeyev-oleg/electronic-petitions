# Profile and Settings

## What You Can Manage
In the Resident App profile area, the beta version is designed to let you:
- view your account email
- switch app language
- review notification preferences

## Language Settings
The app is prepared for:
- English
- Hebrew
- Russian
- Arabic

In the profile area, you can switch the app language.

Future releases will also continue improving:
- right-to-left support for Hebrew and Arabic
- localized help content
- translated feature guidance

## Notification Preferences
The beta profile area includes notification preference placeholders for:
- email notifications
- push notifications

These settings prepare the app for later backend-connected notification behavior.

## Beta Notes
- Some preferences may currently act as UI-level settings only.
- Real backend-linked profile synchronization may be added later.
- Secure account and verification settings will be expanded in later releases.
